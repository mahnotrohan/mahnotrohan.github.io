Hi I'm Rohan! This website is my place to think, somewhere between a journal and a conversation, where ideas don't have to be finished to be worth writing down.

I was born in Bikaner, raised in Delhi, and studied at IIT Kanpur. These days I live in Bangalore and work as a Product Manager at Meesho. Before that, I was a Data Scientist at Microsoft. 

Outside of work, I cook a lot, brew coffee, read memoirs, and run — I've been running regularly for about five years now.

This is my digital garden: a place where I write about the things I notice, the questions I sit with, and the slow process of making sense of things as they take shape.
